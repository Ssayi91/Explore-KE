Tour Lamu

Lamu was once a major trading center, with merchants from Persia, Arabia, and Europe visiting the island to trade goods such as gold, spices, and slaves. Today, the town is a testament to its rich history, with many historic sites and landmarks still standing, including the Lamu Fort, the German Post Office, and the Lamu Museum.
